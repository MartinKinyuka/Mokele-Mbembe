The Mokele-Mbembe

My little lizard is known as the Mokele-Mbembe. She originates from the rivers of the Congo basin and is close relatives with the Loch Ness Monster of Scotland. Distant relatives include apatosaurus and the thunder lizard itself Brontosaurus.

Although she is huge in size, she is a big softie and is a strict vegan. her favourite snacks are melons and her favourite toy is any suitable log she can find in her pond.

Favourite activities

  1. Swimming
  2. Thunder-clapping
  3. Underwater tag
